Our Animals

Mammals

Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. They are social animals that live in groups called prides.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence and strong social bonds.

Tiger

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world, known for their striking orange coat with black stripes.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, recognized for their long necks and legs, and unique spotted coat.

Birds

Parrot

Parrots are known for their vibrant colors and ability to mimic sounds. They are highly intelligent birds.

Eagle

Eagles are powerful birds of prey known for their keen eyesight and strong hunting skills.

Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds that are excellent swimmers, known for their distinctive black and white plumage.

Flamingo

Flamingos are wading birds famous for their pink feathers and long legs, often found in shallow waters.

Reptiles

Crocodile

Crocodiles are large aquatic reptiles known for their powerful jaws and stealthy hunting techniques.

Snake

Snakes are elongated reptiles that can be found in a variety of habitats. Some are venomous, while others are not.

Iguana

Iguanas are large lizards known for their distinctive dewlap and long tails, often found in tropical areas.

Tortoise

Tortoises are land-dwelling reptiles characterized by their hard shells and slow movement.
